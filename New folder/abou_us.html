About Us

HandStations.co.uk

HandStations.co.uk is a newly established supplier of automatic hand sanitiser machines and refill cartridges. Despite being new to the market, we have a proud history of supplying quality products to support hand hygiene.

  • Why Us

    Our first business, Hillside Hand Dryers, was founded in 2016 to supply and service Dyson Airblade™ hand dryers for UK businesses. Today, Hillside Hand Dryers is renowned for its speed, quality of service and value for money – backed by a 4.8 out of 5-star rating based on 161 reviews on Trustpilot.

    As part of our commitment to improve the standard of hand hygiene, HandStations.co.uk was established. Our promise is simple: to make hand hygiene safe, easy and accessible everywhere. To achieve this, we supply quality products from reputable brands you know and trust, and provide ongoing after-sales support to ensure your complete satisfaction.

  • Convenience and reliability lie at the heart of our product range. The HandStation Elite and HandStation Eco range of hand sanitiser machines are automatic, touch-free, easy to assemble, and covered by a 2-year guarantee. Meanwhile, our liquid hand sanitiser refill alcohol-free option – come from Nilaqua®, an award-winning brand and part of the Waterless Limited Company. Our Alcohol based hand sanitiser is our own brand by HandStations.co.uk and available in Liquid or Gel format with the liquid being 80% Alcohol and the Gel 70% Alcohol all of our Santiser products are made in Great Britain.

    As a family owned and operate business, we pride ourselves on building long-term relationships, based on a foundation of trust. You’ll enjoy working directly with down-toearth people who love what they do and who are are committed to exceeding your expectations. Plus, you’ll benefit from the same consistently high level of service the first time, every time.
